My challenge in my present company was this, develop a team that does coding for fun. Fortunately, the rules of recruiting for it are simple, recruit some one who likes to solve puzzles or play games or something like that.

So how to build a fun centric team

1. Recruit a person who you know

2. Recruit some one who codes well

3. Recruit a explorer

4. Stay away from money

5. Never go against your people

How to have a fun centric culture

1. Bash the customer

2. Ignore management

3. Make your team science centric

Say no is no

4. Slash red tape
